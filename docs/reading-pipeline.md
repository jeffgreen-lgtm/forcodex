# CosmoScope Reading Pipeline

## Purpose

CosmoScope separates astronomical facts, astrological interpretation, editorial philosophy, and prose generation into distinct layers.

This protects astrological integrity while allowing AI to produce warm, readable, emotionally intelligent writing.

---

# Layer 1 — Astronomical Facts

Inputs include:

- natal chart
- current planetary positions
- aspects
- houses
- transits
- retrogrades
- progressions (future)
- eclipses
- lunar phases

These are objective facts.

AI does not interpret these.

---

# Layer 2 — Astrological Interpretation

This layer converts facts into structured meaning.

Example:

Transit:  
Mercury square Saturn

Output:

Theme:  
Communication friction

Confidence:  
High

Potential manifestations:

- delays
- editing
- review
- misunderstandings
- patience

Supporting advice:

Slow down.

Clarify.

Review.

Do not catastrophize.

AI does not invent meanings.

Interpretations come from CosmoScope's interpretation manual.

---

# Layer 3 — Prioritization

Determine:

Primary Theme

Secondary Theme

Opportunity

Challenge

Growth Area

Most Important Transit

Ignore weak influences.

Avoid overwhelming users.

---

# Layer 4 — Editorial Philosophy

Apply the CosmoScope Canon.

Every reading must:

prepare

never predict

increase agency

reduce anxiety

remain honest about uncertainty

never exaggerate

---

# Layer 5 — Writing

Only now does Gemini write.

Gemini receives:

birth chart summary

structured interpretation

priority themes

timeframe

CosmoScope Canon

Gemini's responsibility is NOT astrology.

Gemini's responsibility is:

clarity

emotional resonance

storytelling

actionability

voice

---

# Quality Checklist

Every reading should satisfy:

✓ Astrologically faithful

✓ Editorially consistent

✓ Easy to understand

✓ Emotionally useful

✓ Ends with one practical action

✓ Never removes agency

---

# Company Principle

Beautiful writing can never compensate for inaccurate astrology.

Accurate astrology deserves beautiful writing.
