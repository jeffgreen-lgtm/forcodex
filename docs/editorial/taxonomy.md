## Purpose

This taxonomy organizes readings into human domains and weather types so editorial output stays recognizable and practically useful.

These mappings are editorial organization only. They must never invent astrological meaning or override the interpretation layer.

## Example Mapping Patterns

These are editorial mapping examples, not astrological rules.

- A communication-heavy interpretation may map to `Communication -> Clarification`.
- A transit emphasizing emotional reactivity in close bonds may map to `Relationships -> Projection` or `Relationships -> Repair`.
- A concentrated work-pressure interpretation may map to `Work -> Prioritization` or `Work -> Maintenance`.
- A diffuse or fatigued pattern may map to `Rest -> Recovery` or `Rest -> Slowing`.
- A recurring lesson pattern may map to `Growth -> Repetition`.
- A financially cautious interpretation may map to `Money -> Review` or `Money -> Restraint`.

## Usage Rule

Taxonomy should help organize the reading after astrological interpretation is already established.

Taxonomy should never be used to reverse-engineer astrology, create fake certainty, or generate meaning that the interpretation layer did not provide.
