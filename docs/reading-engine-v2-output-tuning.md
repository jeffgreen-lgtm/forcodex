# CosmoScope Reading Engine v2 Output Tuning

## Purpose

This document is where we tune CosmoScope Reading Engine v2 outputs before connecting a real AI provider.

The goal is not just to make the readings sound beautiful.

The goal is to make every reading show the member the path toward their most aligned self.

## Current Brand Spine

CosmoScope reads the stars, then shows you the path toward your most aligned self.

A reading should not just tell the member what is happening. It should name the pattern, reveal the pressure point, and show the path forward.

## Founder Taste Notes

CosmoScope should feel:
- intimate
- elegant
- direct
- useful
- emotionally intelligent
- lightly mystical
- grounded in agency
- premium enough to save, screenshot, or reread

CosmoScope should not feel:
- generic
- copied from another astrology brand
- snarky for the sake of snark
- fake mystical
- overly therapeutic
- like toxic positivity
- like manifestation without action
- like AI filler

## Output Scoring Rubric

Score every reading from 1 to 5 in each area.

### 1. Specificity

Does it use the actual member name, Sun, Moon, Rising, transit, and timeframe naturally?

### 2. Path Forward

Does it show the member how to move toward their most aligned self?

### 3. Pressure Point

Does it clearly name the tension, friction, or focus lens?

### 4. Agency

Does it preserve the member’s power to choose?

### 5. Voice

Does it sound like CosmoScope and not like another astrology app?

### 6. Usefulness

Could the member actually do something with this today or this week?

### 7. Screenshot Value

Would someone save it, screenshot it, or send it to a friend?

## Current Daily Sample

Jeff, the pattern underneath the pattern today is that your instinct may be moving faster than your clarity. Your Sagittarius Sun wants to find the larger truth and move toward a life with more meaning, but the stronger move is not more speed. It is choosing the one truth that can survive contact with the actual day.

Your Libra Moon is the private weather system underneath the obvious story. It needs balance, reciprocity, and enough peace to hear yourself clearly. If that need is ignored, the day can turn ordinary friction into a false emergency; if it is respected, you get your leverage back before the noise makes decisions for you.

Your Pisces Rising is the doorway other people meet first. Today, porous intuition works best when it is edited, not performed. Let the room feel your signal without giving the room access to your whole nervous system.

Venus in Leo pressing on your Mercury gives the day its pressure point. This is not a warning; it is a focus lens. Watch where you want instant certainty, because that is probably where the deeper pattern is asking for a cleaner container.

**Your move:** Pick one place where you are tempted to over-explain. Say the truer thing in fewer words, then let the result give you information.

## Daily Revision Direction

The daily reading should feel like a clean morning signal.

Recommended shape:
1. Name the day’s core pattern.
2. Explain the Sun/Moon/Rising roles.
3. Name the transit pressure point.
4. Show the aligned path.
5. End with one practical move.

## Current Weekly Sample

The week’s actual story is not the loudest event. It is the sequence underneath it, and Jeff, your best read comes from tracking how your Sagittarius Sun, your Libra Moon, and your Pisces Rising each ask for a different kind of honesty.

Early in the week, your Sagittarius Sun wants to find the larger truth and move toward a life with more meaning. That can be powerful, but only if you stop confusing motion with alignment. The first win is not a dramatic leap; it is refusing to spend your life force on a decision that has not earned it.

Midweek, your Libra Moon becomes the truth serum. It needs balance, reciprocity, and enough peace to hear yourself clearly, and the body will probably say that before the mind admits it. Notice what gets louder when you are tired, rushed, or trying to keep everyone else comfortable.

By the end of the week, your Pisces Rising becomes the re-entry point. Let porous intuition help you return to the world with more signal and less residue. You do not need to drag the whole emotional weather system into every room.

Venus in Leo pressing on your Mercury marks the week’s pressure point. The lesson is not to avoid friction. The lesson is to stop treating every friction point as proof that you are behind, wrong, or required to perform.

**Your move:** Choose the repeating situation that keeps asking for your attention. Decide whether it needs action, a boundary, or simply less performance from you.
